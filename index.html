Guide:

1) Click anywhere on Mariah until you hear one of her whistle notes

2) Press any key between 1 and 9 to change whistle note

3) Click in different places on Mariah using different whistle notes to give angel-nn some training data

4) When you are ready, press the 't' key and wait for angel-nn to learn Mariah's notes

5) Now when you click on Mariah you don't need to select a 1-9 number, click anywhere, angel-nn will predict what note should play based on where you clicked the training data


Notes:

a)angel-nn currently only works on Chrome

The click-noises during training data are from an actual Mariah Carey recording. The predicted notes after training are generated using the p5.js sound library by matching frequencies
